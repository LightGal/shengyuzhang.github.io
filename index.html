Biography

Currently I am a 5th year Ph.D candidate of the College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Fei Wu. I was so lucky to work with Prof. Zhou Zhao and Prof. Kun Kuang at Zhejiang University.

From March 2021, I become a visiting research scholar of NExT++ Research Center, National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Tat-seng Chua. I feel grateful to work with Prof. Fuli Feng at University of Science and Technology of China.

I have over 20 publications appeared in several top-tier conferences such as KDD, SIGIR, WWW, and ACMMM, and journals including TKDE. My research interests include information retrieval and multi-media analysis, particularly in machine learning techniques and applications such as causal inference, edge-cloud collaborative learning, graph neural networks, and recommender systems. Research models have been deployed in recommender systems of Mobile Taobao, Huwei Music, and Tencent News.
