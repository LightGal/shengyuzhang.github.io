Biography

Currently I am a 4th year Ph.D candidate of the College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Wu Fei.
From March 2021, I become a visiting research scholar of NExT++ Research Center, National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Chua Tat-seng.

Research Interests

My research interests include Multi-modal Learning, Recommendation System, and Causal Inference.
